Thermostat starts at 20 degrees
You can increase with the up button
You can decrease with the down button
The minimum temperature is 10 degrees
If power saving is on the max temp is 25 degrees
If power saving is off the max temp is 32 degrees
Powersaving mode is on by default
You can reset to 20 degrees by pressing reset
The thermostat should be colour coded.
After every change, the thermo stat should make a POST request to the local host/temperature_change with the new temp
